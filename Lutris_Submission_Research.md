# Lutris Submission Research

## Submission Requirements
1. Remove external Proton or Wine version use from the installer. Mention tested versions only in "Technical info".
2. Ensure installer compatibility with Lutris Flatpak.

## Moderator Feedback
- Feedback specific to the above requirements will be taken into account to enhance submissions.

## Initial Analysis of Current Installer
- Evaluate what external versions of Proton or Wine are currently being used in the installer.
- Review the aspects of the installer that need adjustment to meet the criteria for removing external dependencies.

## Steps for Meeting Flatpak Compatibility
1. Analyze the existing installer codebase for integrations that may not be Flatpak compatible.
2. Identify dependencies and package structures used within the installer that necessitate modifications for Flatpak.
3. Conduct tests with the current installer setup within a Flatpak environment to outline specific issues and resolutions required for compliance.

## Conclusion
- To achieve compliance with Lutris’ rules, it will be necessary to engage in code modifications that stabilize the installer and ensure compatibility with the Flatpak ecosystem. Further evaluations and tests will refine the submission further.
